Geography Guesser

In this captivating geography-based game, players are tasked with identifying the locations of random satellite photos taken from various iconic spots across the United States. The images come from a diverse selection of sources, including breathtaking aerial views featured in Bill Harris' Landscapes of America and detailed satellite captures of the 30 largest airports in the country. Whether players are zooming in on famous natural wonders or sprawling urban hubs, each photo provides a unique challenge that tests their knowledge of the U.S. landscape.

The game offers an engaging way to explore the geographical diversity of the country, from sweeping deserts and mountain ranges to dense cityscapes and coastal regions. Landcapes of America is a photography book containing 300+ photos of the terrain and scenery across America. The book higlights the diversity and beauty of America both as a whole and state by state. When viewing such photos, it is important to focus on reigonal landmarks, such as bodies of water, and the topography of the area.

This dataset also includes aerial photography of major US airports to further one's appreciation of the scale and extent of air travel within the United States. You'll find that though they share common features, no two airports are exactly alike. Important features to take note of are the runway numbers (ex. 09/27) and terminal design.
